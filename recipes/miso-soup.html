Miso Soup

Description

Full of savory flavors miso soup. With dashi and yellow, white, or red miso paste â€” yellow miso is sweet and creamy, red miso is stronger and saltier.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.

  3. Step 3

    Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.

  4. Step 4

    Stir in tofu.

  5. Step 5

    Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
