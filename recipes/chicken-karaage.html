Chicken Karaage

Description

Delicious chicken karaage recipe for Japanese-style fried chicken flavored with ginger, garlic, sake, and soy sauce. Serve as an appetizer or with rice and veggies to make a yummy meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sake (Japanese rice wine)
  • 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 ½ pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • ¾ cup cornstarch

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Combine soy sauce, sake, and ginger in a large bowl. Add chicken; turn to coat.

    Cover with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  3. Step 3

    Place cornstarch in a large resealable plastic bag.

    Add chicken; seal bag and toss until chicken is coated with cornstarch.

  4. Step 4

    Fry chicken in batches until golden brown and juices run clear, 2 to 3 minutes.

    Drain on paper towels or on a wire rack.
